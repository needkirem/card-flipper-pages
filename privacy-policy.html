Privacy Policy

Card Flipper

Last updated: October 20, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Card Flipper ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect certain information directly from your use of the App, which includes:

  • Technical Information: We collect data related to application performance, crashes, bugs, and other technical issues. This helps us monitor the stability and reliability of the App.
  • Usage Data: We collect analytical events, such as clicks within the App, screen views, and general interaction patterns. This data helps us understand how users engage with the App and allows us to improve the user experience.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version to ensure compatibility and provide better support.

Important: We do NOT collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To analyze and understand how the App is used, allowing us to improve its features, functionality, and overall user experience.
  • To monitor and resolve technical issues, crashes, and bugs, ensuring the App operates smoothly and reliably.
  • To provide customer support and respond to user inquiries.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to assist us in collecting and analyzing the information described above:

  • Firebase: We utilize Google Firebase, specifically Firebase Crashlytics for error reporting and Firebase Analytics for usage data. Firebase collects and processes data in accordance with its own privacy policy. For more information on how Firebase collects and processes data, please visit the Google Firebase Privacy & Security page.
  • Google Play Services: Our app may use Google Play Services for analytics and crash reporting when available on Android devices.

These third parties are obligated not to disclose or use the information for any other purpose.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Management

We retain the collected data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Users do not have direct control or management options over the collected technical and usage data within the App.

7. Children's Privacy

Card Flipper is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that appropriate safeguards are in place.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to delete your personal information
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

This Privacy Policy complies with the requirements of both Google Play Store and Apple App Store regarding data collection and privacy practices for mobile applications.
